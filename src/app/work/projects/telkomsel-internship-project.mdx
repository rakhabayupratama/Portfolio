## Overview

This project was developed during a **2-month internship at Telkomsel** (July-August 2025) as part of the Data Analyst role in the **Asset Capitalization Support** team. The deliverable was an interactive **PO Absorption Monitoring Dashboard** built in Power BI, designed to give the team a real-time view of Purchase Order (PO) capitalization progress, budget utilization, and vendor performance across the organization.

---

## Problem Statement

The Asset Capitalization Support team at Telkomsel was managing PO absorption monitoring through a **manual Excel dashboard** that required regular manual updates, a process that was time-consuming and prone to inconsistency. There was a clear need for an automated, interactive dashboard that could reflect the latest data without manual intervention, and that the team could adopt immediately into their daily workflow.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. PO Absorption (Overview)
The main landing page of the dashboard, providing a high-level summary of overall PO absorption performance. Connected to live data with the last refresh timestamp displayed for transparency.

### 2. PO Capitalization
Tracks the lifecycle of Purchase Orders from release to capitalization. Key components include:
- **PO Released** - total value and count of released purchase orders
- **Goods Receipt (GR)** - GR completion status per PO
- **Top Summary PO by Year** - year-over-year comparison of PO capitalization progress
- Summary cards displaying key metrics across multiple dimensions

### 3. Capex Paper
Compares actual PO data against the approved Capital Expenditure (Capex) budget paper. Key views include:
- **PO vs Capex Paper** - variance analysis between planned budget and actual PO values
- **Capitalization 2025 vs Capex Paper** - progress of 2025 capitalizations relative to budget
- **Balance AUC (Assets Under Construction)** - remaining balance of assets yet to be capitalized

### 4. GR 0 (Zero Goods Receipt)
A focused page highlighting Purchase Orders with no Goods Receipt recorded - indicating potential delays, open commitments, or items requiring follow-up. Displayed as pivot tables for easy filtering by department and vendor.

### 5. Top Ten Remaining Vendor & Fund Center
Ranks the top 10 vendors and fund centers with the highest remaining PO balances, enabling the team to prioritize follow-ups and improve absorption rates before period-end closing.

## Workflow

1. **Data Extraction (SAP)** - Raw procurement and financial data was extracted from **SAP** by the team admin and handed over in Excel format
2. **Data Cleaning (Excel)** - Cleaned and reformatted the data in **Microsoft Excel**, focusing on standardizing date formats and numeric values to match MySQL import requirements
3. **Data Import (MySQL)** - Converted the cleaned Excel file to **CSV** and imported it into **MySQL** as the structured data source
4. **Dashboard Development (Power BI)** - Connected **Power BI** directly to MySQL and built the full interactive dashboard with 5 report pages, including slicers, KPI cards, tables, and charts
5. **Export & Presentation (PowerPoint)** - Exported the completed dashboard into **PowerPoint** format for final presentation and documentation to the team

---

## Tools & Technologies

- **SAP** - source system for raw procurement and asset data (extracted by admin)
- **Microsoft Excel** - data cleaning, date and numeric formatting for MySQL compatibility
- **MySQL** - structured data storage and querying after CSV import
- **Power BI** - interactive dashboard design, data modeling, and visualization
- **PowerPoint** - final export and presentation of dashboard results

---

## Key Outcomes

- **Replaced the manual Excel dashboard** with a fully automated, interactive Power BI dashboard that the team adopted directly into their workflow
- Enabled the Asset Capitalization Support team to monitor PO absorption status without manual data updates
- Surfaced the top vendors and fund centers with pending balances, supporting targeted follow-ups before period-end closing
- Improved visibility into GR 0 items, helping the team identify and resolve open commitments more efficiently
